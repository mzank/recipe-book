# Mixed Nuts 

This recipe contains mixed nuts.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup almonds
- 1/2 cup cashews
- 1/2 cup walnuts
- 1/2 cup peanuts
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp salt


## Instructions

- preheat oven to 180°C
- add nuts to a bowl
- drizzle with olive oil
- add salt
- mix well
- spread nuts on a baking tray
- roast for 10–12 minutes
- let cool slightly
- enjoy!
